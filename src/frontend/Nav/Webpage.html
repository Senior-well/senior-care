Elder Care Management App

The Elder Care Management App is a comprehensive tool designed to assist families in managing the care of their aging loved ones. It offers a range of features that ensure the health, safety, and social well-being of the elderly, while providing families with peace of mind.

Medication & Health Monitoring

Get reminders for medications, track doctor's appointments, and monitor vital signs like blood pressure and glucose levels.

Caregiver Coordination

Manage schedules between family members and professional caregivers, ensuring proper communication and coordination.

Emergency Alerts

Instant notifications for family members or caregivers in case of emergencies, ensuring timely response and support.

Mental & Physical Exercises

Engage the elderly with games, memory exercises, and fitness routines to improve mental health and physical well-being.
